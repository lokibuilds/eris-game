# ERIS — Virtual Angel Flight 🩷

A flappy bird-style browser game made for the ERIS comeback.

Play as a Virtual Angel escaping Artemis' control — dodge her pillars, survive her Eye, and keep flying.

---

## How to Play

- **Tap / Space bar** to flap and fly
- Dodge the purple Artemis pillars
- Survive the **Artemis Eye** — it tracks you and crosses the screen. don't let it catch you!
- Every **20 points**, speed increases
- The Eye gets faster the longer you survive
